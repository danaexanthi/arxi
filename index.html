Managed incorporation

Greece or Cyprus —
we handle everything

Two of Europe's most strategically positioned jurisdictions for founders. We manage the entire process, so you remain focused on building.

01

Strategy & jurisdiction selection

We align your business model, investor profile, and tax objectives with the optimal legal structure — IKE or EPE in Greece, or Ltd in Cyprus.

02

Company name search & reservation

We verify availability and reserve your company name with GEMI (Greece) or the Cyprus Registrar of Companies.

03

Document preparation

Drafting of Articles of Association, shareholder agreements, and all ancillary incorporation documents — tailored to your requirements.

04

Notarial filing & registry registration

We coordinate all notarial appointments and statutory filings on your behalf. Physical presence is not required in most cases.

05

Tax registration

Registration with AADE (Greece) or the Cyprus Tax Department, including VAT registration where applicable.

06

Banking & operational setup

Introductions to banking partners, registered address options, and guidance on your first statutory compliance obligations.

Compare jurisdictions

Greece and Cyprus each carry distinct strategic advantages. We help you select the structure that best fits your business model and future plans.

IKE — Ιδιωτική Κεφαλαιουχική Εταιρεία

Minimum capital: €1 · Timeline: 3–5 business days via GEMI online · Corporate tax: 22% · Fully digital incorporation available · Ideal for: EU-facing product companies, Greek market entry, Balkan gateway structuring.
Private Limited Company (Ltd)

Minimum capital: €1 · Timeline: 5–10 business days · Corporate tax: 12.5% (among the EU's lowest) · IP Box regime: 2.5% effective rate on qualifying IP income · Ideal for: IP holding structures, international tax planning, fintech licensing, and MiCA-compliant entities.
